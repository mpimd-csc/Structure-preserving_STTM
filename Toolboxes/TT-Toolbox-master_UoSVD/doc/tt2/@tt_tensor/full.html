full

PURPOSE

Converts TT-tensor to the full tensor

SYNOPSIS

function [a] = full(tt, sizes)

DESCRIPTION 

Converts TT-tensor to the full tensor
   [A]=FULL(TT) --- full tensor from TT-tensor

   [A]=FULL(TT,SIZES) --- full tensor from TT-tensor reshaped into
