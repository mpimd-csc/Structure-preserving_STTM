kron

PURPOSE

Kronecker product of two TT-matrices

SYNOPSIS

function [c] = kron(a,b)

DESCRIPTION 

Kronecker product of two TT-matrices
   [C]=KRON(A,B) Kronecker product of two TT-matrices. One of the
   arguments can be empty. In this case, the other, nonempty argument is
   returned
