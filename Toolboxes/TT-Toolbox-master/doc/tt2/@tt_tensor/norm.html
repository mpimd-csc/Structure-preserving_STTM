norm

PURPOSE

Frobenius norm of the TT-tensor

SYNOPSIS

function [nrm] = norm(tt)

DESCRIPTION 

Frobenius norm of the TT-tensor
   [NRM]=NORM(TT) Based on the QR-decomposition, has O(nu) accuracy
